easy salsa roja

  • time: 1 hr
  • serves: 10
ingredients quantity
red tomatos 4-6
onion 3/4
chili pepper 2-3
garlic 5-8 cloves
seasonings to taste

step 1: preheat oven to 420F. halve tomatoes and quarter onions. slice peppers on one side only (this makes them easy to deseed later). leave garlic cloves whole.

step 2: line baking sheet with foil and oil. place your veggies on sheet, spray with more oil if you wish, and season. put in oven for 40-45 minutes.

step 3: take veggies out when onions have crisped, slightly burnt corners. let cool 5 mins, then chop onions loosely. deseed/derib peppers (carefully!), and chop. skin garlic.

step 4: using tongs, put all veggies in a blender/processor, add limon juice + more seasonings. pulse until desired consistency is reached. enjoy on tortilla chips, quesadillas, huevos rancheros, + mas!

suggested seasonings: cumin, black pepper, italian seasoning, rosemary, mexican oregano

pb ramen

  • time: 10 mins
  • serves: 1
ingredients quantity
ramen 1 pkg
peanut butter 2 tbs
low sodium soy sauce 1 tbs
rice vinegar 1 tbs
garlic 1-2 cloves or 1 tsp
ginger 1 cm or 1/2 tsp
hot sauce to taste
sesame oil dash

step 1: boil water. while water boils, prepare peanut butter sauce in serving bowl (all ingredients except ramen). don't mix! (the pb clumps harder if you mix early)

step 2: once water is boiling, add ramen. cook according to package.

step 3: once ramen is cooked, add 1/2 cup of hot water from the ramen to your serving bowl. now mix!

step 4: drain ramen and add to bowl. enjoy!

topping options: sesame seeds, seaweed, chili flakes, green onion

egg avocado bagel

  • time: 15 mins
  • serves: 1
ingredients quantity
bagel 1/2 (one side)
egg 1
avocado 1/2
grated cheese (optional) 2-3 tbs

step 1: if using cheese, sprinkle on bagel. toast bagel to desired crispiness. (if you don't have a toaster oven, sprinkle cheese on bagel after it's toasted).

step 2: cook egg in frying pan (over-medium is scrumptious). slice avocado while egg cooks.

step 3: layer sliced avocado on toasted bagel/cheese. add egg on top of the avocado. enjoy!

top with pepper, garlic salt, turmeric
